FORT IRWIN UPDATE

Wednesday, Dec. 16, 2015

3:30 p.m.

GRIM HOLIDAYS FOR SOME. An average of 6 Soldiers across the Army are killed in off duty accident during the holiday season.  Last year, 5 Soldiers were killed in privately owned vehicle accidents.  Over half of these accidents involved alcohol.  Increased traffic, inclement weather, and impaired driving due to distracted driving, alcohol or fatigue make the holiday season the most dangerous time to drive.    Before hosting a party and you are serving alcohol, plan well in advance for designated drivers. Have back-up plans that include, taxis, or sleeping arrangements. Make sure that non-alcoholic beverages are available.

BOWLING CENTER CLOSED FOR RENOVATION. Due to major renovations, Strike Zone Bowling Center was closed on Nov. 26 and will reopen in Aug. 2015.
